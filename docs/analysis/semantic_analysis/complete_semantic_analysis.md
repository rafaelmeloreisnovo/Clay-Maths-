## Terminology Glossary

### Technical Terms

**Mahalanobis Distance:**
- **Definition:** Distance normalized by covariance
- **Context:** Measures similarity accounting for correlations
- **Usage:** Cluster assignment criterion
- **Semantic field:** Statistics, pattern recognition, geometry

**Median Absolute Deviation (MAD):**
- **Definition:** Robust measure of statistical dispersion
- **Context:** Outlier-resistant alternative to standard deviation
- **Usage:** Robust clipping in preprocessing
- **Semantic field:** Robust statistics, data cleaning

**Exponential Moving Average (EMA):**
- **Definition:** Weighted average giving more importance to recent data
- **Context:** Time series analysis, adaptive estimation
- **Usage:** Cluster centroid and covariance updates
- **Semantic field:** Signal processing, finance, online learning

**Friedmann Equation:**
- **Definition:** Governs cosmic expansion in GR
- **Context:** Fundamental equation in cosmology
- **Usage:** Extended with plasma and magnetic terms
- **Semantic field:** Cosmology, differential equations, general relativity
